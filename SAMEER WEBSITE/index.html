About Us:

SAAF STEEL was founded in 2015, as a quality step to steel industry in Almadinah Almunawarah. The factory has been supplied with the most evolutionary and modern machinery and combined with a group of skilled workers, who work side by side to present one of the highest possible quality.
We have an ambition to be One of the best industrialist in efficiency and productivity as well as environment, safety and health measures.
Our efforts together with those of other equally sincere nationals would certainly lead our beloved country to take its position among developed countries both industrial and technological.
